About our startup

We’re a Sydney-based startup founded by four high school girls who believe finding part-time work should be simple, fair, and accessible for students. Our platform connects students with flexible opportunities that fit around school and life.

Our mission

Empower high school students with real-world experience by making job discovery transparent, local, and student-friendly—while helping small businesses find reliable, motivated talent.

Our story

What started as a school project quickly grew into a community-focused platform. We talked to students, parents, and local businesses across Sydney, and built a solution that’s lightweight, mobile-first, and easy to use. Every feature is designed around student needs.

Awards and recognition

First place — Spark Tank

Recognized for innovation, clarity of vision, and practical impact for student employment. This win helped us refine our roadmap and accelerate our prototype.

Third place — Macquarie University Incubator program

Celebrated for strong user research and a scalable approach. The incubator’s mentorship guided our product validation and community partnerships.
